About CHOLBAM

What is CHOLBAM?

CHOLBAM is a medication used to treat bile acid synthesis disorders due to single enzyme defects and as adjunctive treatment of peroxisomal disorders.

How does CHOLBAM work?

CHOLBAM works by replacing bile acids that are missing in patients with bile acid synthesis disorders.

What results have been seen with CHOLBAM?

In 11 of 12 patients with SLOS given CHOLBAM 10 mg/kg/day and cholesterol supplementation for 2 months, in an open-label, pilot study. Long-term studies are needed to determine durability of effect and possible clinical benefits.

SLOS

What is SLOS?

Smith-Lemli-Opitz Syndrome (SLOS) is a rare genetic disorder characterized by multiple congenital abnormalities, intellectual disability, and behavioral problems. It is caused by a deficiency in the enzyme 7-dehydrocholesterol reductase, which is needed to produce cholesterol.

Patient Support

Mirum Access Plus is a patient support program designed to help patients access CHOLBAM therapy and provide resources for patients and caregivers.
